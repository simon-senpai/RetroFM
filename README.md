# RetroFM

A personal AI FM radio station. Distils your music taste into a living radio experience — curated by an AI DJ named **Sine**, powered by your 10 years of NetEase Music listening history.

Inspired by [Claudio FM](https://mmguo.dev/claudio-fm/) by mmguo.

---

## What it is

RetroFM is a desktop app that runs a private radio station tuned entirely to you. It learns your taste, generates daily program blocks, and has Sine — your AI DJ — introduce each program with a spoken commentary. Between programs, you can chat with Sine in text and she'll adjust the queue to match your mood.

It's not an algorithm. It has taste.

---

## How it works

### The AI DJ — Sine

Sine is a female AI DJ with a thoughtful, slightly melancholy personality. She speaks only when a new program starts — introducing the block with a short piece of audio commentary (generated by GPT-4o, voiced by OpenAI TTS). Between songs, she's available to chat in text: ask her to shift the mood, find something sadder, or explain why she picked a track.

### The taste profile — `taste.md`

On first run, RetroFM pulls your full NetEase Music listening history (10+ years of play counts, liked songs, collected playlists) and feeds it to GPT-4o to generate `taste.md` — a rich markdown document describing your musical personality: genres, moods, recurring artists, time-of-day patterns, seasonal cycles.

`taste.md` is a living document. It updates automatically:
- **Weekly**: Re-fetches NetEase play-count data and notes drift (what you've been listening to more or less)
- **Per session**: Tracks likes, skips, and chat topics, then proposes small updates to reflect how your taste is evolving

You can open and read `taste.md` anytime — it's a genuinely interesting artefact.

### The music pool

RetroFM builds a large, constantly refreshing pool of music from:
- Your liked songs (~400)
- Songs from your collected playlists (thousands)
- NetEase's daily personalised recommendations (already know your 10yr history)
- Top tracks from similar artists to ones you love

Sine curates from this pool — not from the whole internet, not from a genre tag. From music that already belongs to your world.

### Two-way NetEase sync

When you ❤️ a song in RetroFM, it writes back to your NetEase liked list. Your RetroFM likes show up in NetEase, and vice versa.

## The interface

RetroFM runs as a fixed phone-size window — portrait, no resizing. Dark mode only.

**Station View** (default):
- Live dot-matrix clock — you're always tuned in
- Thin player bar — controls are minimal, the content is the star
- Named program queue (Sine gives each block a title)
- Chat history — full conversation with Sine, text only during playback

**Speaking View** (when Sine starts a new program):
- Full-width voice waveform across the canvas
- White card slides up from the bottom
- AI-generated episode title for the moment (large, bold — like a book cover)
- Timestamped transcript builds as she speaks

## Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Desktop app | Electron |
| Backend | Node.js + Express |
| Music data | NeteaseCloudMusicApi (unofficial) |
| AI — curation & chat | OpenAI GPT-4o |
| AI — quick chat replies | OpenAI GPT-4o-mini |
| DJ voice | OpenAI TTS (`tts-1-hd`, `shimmer`) |
| Frontend | Vanilla JS, HTML/CSS |
| Fonts | Martian Mono (UI) + IBM Plex Mono (chat/DJ text) |

---

## Design

- **Palette**: Midnight Ink — deep blue-black (`#06060e`), indigo accents
- **Logo**: ✳ purple eight-point star
- **Clock**: Custom CSS dot-matrix (4×7 grid per digit, updates live)
- **Fixed canvas**: ~390×844px, phone proportions, no responsive layout
